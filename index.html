H.O.P.E's Mission and vision

The foundation is designed, to take care of our precious elderly. The aim is for these elders to experience life as long and enjoyable as possible in their final phases of life. It is a 24-hour care facility, from a Dutch standard view. The foundation wants to brush up the knowlegde and skills of the Aruban nurses by training and given them up to date materials. Hope has of today location one in Tanki Leendert

Meet the Directors of H.O.P.E Foundation

Miranda Trimon is a jolly person and has worked almost her entire life in the nursing industry and at one point she was incharge of her own department. She enjoys her line of work and is passionate about it. In 2016 she moved with her husband to the small Island of Aruba and took over the HOPE foundation in Tanki Leendert.

The director is in fact a Dutch who re-started this foundation since August 2016. She wants to buy materials, as is customary in the Netherlands to make the foundation unique.

Oswald Trimon is a energetic person and a passion for food. He was born on Aruba and lived for the last 16 years in Holland. In 2016 he moved back to Aruba with his wife, because his heart lies on Aruba. As a professional he is a chef, this is also his role at both nursing homes.
